About Us

CollegePortal is a student-first platform designed to streamline academic resources, mentorship, and career opportunities. Our mission is to empower every student with tools to succeed inside and beyond the classroom.

Founded by educators and technologists, we believe in accessible, modular, and community-driven learning experiences.
